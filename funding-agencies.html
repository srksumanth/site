Research​ ​Project​ ​Funding​ ​Agencies

Following is a list of funding agencies for sanction of Research and Development (R&D) projects.

  • Department of Electronics and Information Technology
  • Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB)
  • Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO)
  • Atomic Energy Regulatory Board (AERB)
  • Aeronautics Research and Development Board (ARDB)
  • Board of Research in Nuclear Sciences (BRNS)
  • Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CSIR)
  • Department of Atomic Energy (DAE)
  • Department of Biotechnology (DBT)
  • Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR)
  • Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR)
  • Indian Council of Social Science Research (ICSSR)
  • Indian National Science Academy (INSA)
  • Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
  • Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE)
  • Ministry of Environment & Forests (MoEF)
  • Ministry of Defence
  • Ministry of Earth Science
  • Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
  • Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas
  • Ministry of Rural Development
  • Ministry of Railways
  • Department of Chemicals & Petrochemicals, Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers
  • National Board for Higher Mathematics (NBHM)
  • Naval Research Board (NRB)
  • Petroleum Conservation Research Association (PCRA)
  • Tata Institute of Fundamental Research

Note:There may be more number of funding agencies and more number of schemes for R&D projects, which the faculty members are requested to individually make efforts and apply. The information given below is not complete and may change. Thus the faculty members are also requested to visit the respective funding agencies websites for detailed and updated information.
